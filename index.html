  2. deforestation-Deforestation refers to the decrease in forest areas across the world that are lost for other uses such as agricultural croplands, urbanization, or mining activities.

    What if the earth gets warmer?

    1. If the earth gets warmer by 4 degrees we will be looking at a vastly changed planet.     Facts
    1. The Arctic is one of the worst places to be affected by global warming.
    2. The Arctic ice is melting rapidly. By 2040 the region is expected to have a completely ice-free summer or even earlier.
    3. Sea levels have risen about 7 inches in the last 100 years, which is more than the previous 2000 years combined. The rising sea levels due to global warming could threaten the lives of people living along with the coastal areas.
    4. As per NASA, “the global average surface temperature rose 0.6 to 0.9 degrees Celsius (1.1 to 1.6° F) between 1906 and 2005, and the rate of temperature increase has nearly doubled in the last 50 years. Temperatures are certain to go up further.”
